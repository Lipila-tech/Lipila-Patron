Getting Started
Is it free to create a Luso account?

Absolutely! Creating a Luso account is completely free. We want to make it as easy as possible for you to start earning from your creative work.

How do my patrons pay me?

Luso integrates seamlessly with Zambia's mobile money platforms. Your patrons can easily pay you using their mobile money accounts for a convenient and secure transaction.

How do fans become patrons?

Your fans can become patrons by signing up as a patron and simply click join on your profile.

Fees & Transactions

We do charge a small commission fee on each transaction processed through Lipila Patron. This fee helps us maintain and improve the platform to better serve both creators and patrons.

Currently, Luso supports Zambian Kwacha (ZMW) transactions. We are constantly working on expanding our supported currencies in the future.
